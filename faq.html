Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

+

Typically, it will take us a week or less to build you a single page website, however it may vary depending on your requirements.

Can I update the website content myself?

+

Our sites are custom-built; no WordPress or anything like that. Because of this, most clients prefer for us to handle updates for them.

For $24 per month, we can revise your website twice per month.

What information do you need to get started?

+

We'll need basic information about your business, your goals for the website, any content you want to include (text, images, logos), and examples of websites you like. We'll guide you through everything during our initial consultation.

Can you redesign my existing website?

+

Of course. We can either redesign your existing website or build a completely new one from scratch.

Do you provide refunds?

+

Once you have paid, we don't provide refunds. However, during the review stage, you don't have to pay until you're completely satisfied with the design. If you don't like it, you don't pay.

How do the revisions work?

+

With our maintenance package, you get two revisions per month included. This includes minor content updates, design tweaks and text changes. It does not include major redesigns or adding new pages. Most updates are done within 1-3 days. Unused revisions do not roll over to the next month.
