Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

+

Typically, it will take me 1-2 weeks to build you a single page website, however it may vary depending on your requirements and it can often be completed in less than a week. Pages with 2-5 pages may take longer.

What is your pricing structure?

+

For a simple, single-page website, we charge $349. For a 5 page website, the price is $1,299. For custom projects, please contact us for a detailed quote. Maintenance costs $24 per month.

Do you provide hosting and domain services?

+

Yes! We handle all technical aspects including domain registration, hosting setup, and configuration. We understand that for most people managing these technical details is confusing.

Can I update the website content myself?

+

We provide maintenance to your website, so you don't have to worry about updates or technical issues. If you want to make changes yourself, we can discuss options for easy content management.

Will my website work on mobile devices?

+

Yes! All our websites are fully responsive and optimized for all devices.

What happens after my website is launched?

+

After launch, we provide ongoing support for any technical issues or questions. We can also help with updates, maintenance, and future enhancements as your business grows.

Do you offer SEO (search engine optimization) services?

+

Yes, all of our websites are built with SEO in mind.

What information do you need to get started?

+

We'll need basic information about your business, your goals for the website, any content you want to include (text, images, logos), and examples of websites you like. We'll guide you through everything during our initial consultation.

Can you redesign my existing website?

+

Of course. We can either redesign your existing website or build a completely new one from scratch.

Do you provide website maintenance?

+

Yes! We offer ongoing maintenance packages to keep your website running smoothly, including updates, backups, security monitoring, and content changes. Contact us to discuss your maintenance needs.
